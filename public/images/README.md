# Profile Image Guide

## Where to put your headshot:

Place your profile photo in this directory as:
- `profile.jpg` (recommended) OR
- `profile.png`

## Image Specifications:

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Dimensions**: Square image, minimum 400x400px (800x800px recommended for retina displays)
- **File size**: Keep under 500KB for fast loading
- **Background**: Professional background or clean backdrop
- **Cropping**: Headshot or head-and-shoulders shot works best
