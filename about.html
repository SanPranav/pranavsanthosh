My Journey

My journey in technology began with curiosity and has evolved into a passion for creating meaningful change through innovation. From my early days experimenting with code to now teaching the next generation of programmers, every step has reinforced my belief that technology should serve humanity.


Through my work at SDSC Team Optix, I've discovered the joy of making complex programming concepts accessible to young learners. The 4:30 PM Python sessions aren't just about syntax and algorithms - they're about nurturing computational thinking and problem-solving skills that will serve these students throughout their lives.


My involvement in environmental conservation and scouting has shaped my leadership philosophy. Whether it's extracting shopping carts from Del Mar beaches or guiding fellow scouts through challenging terrain, I've learned that effective leadership combines technical competence with empathy, persistence, and a genuine commitment to collective success.


Looking ahead, I'm excited about the convergence of technology and social impact. My goal is to continue developing innovative solutions that not only showcase technical excellence but also address real-world challenges in education, environmental conservation, and community development.
